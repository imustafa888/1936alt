################################################################
###BEF PREPARE
################################################################
# Rationale behind time and IC costs:
# 1. free_ic * standard time subtracts base IC rather than effective IC, which is almost always higher than the base IC by at least 20% to 25%. As a consequence, all ic costs are reduced by 25%. 1 infantry division therefore costs 3.75 IC instead of 5 IC.
# 2. free_ic * standard time assumes equal cost for each division, which is not true for serial builds where cost decreases for every new division
# 3. If serialization is implemented for BEF divisions, it would incentivise the British to continue pumping troops into the European theatre, which is the wrong focus - Britain should be focusing on the American theatre. The human British player can continue to create troops as part of the BEF program and then transport them to the Americas anyway, but the British AI cannot really do that, hence it would create an unfair advantage to the British player over the AI.
# 4. The cost of 1 unserialized infantry division is about 225 days, while the cost of completely serialized (-35%) infantry division is about 146 days. 
# 5. To create the incentive for BEF formation but reduce the incentive for indefinite BEF spamming, instead of a progressive reduction in time, the time-cost of 1 BEF infantry division is fixed at 180 days which is equivalent to -20% serialization bonus, meaning that pumping out more BEF divisions becomes relatively unprofitable after the 5th one, a better bonus can be reached through standard seralized builds.
# 6. If UK starts creating BEF in January 1937, by the expected outbreak of war in mid-1939, UK can be expected to have produced exactly 5 infantry divisions for every theatre. Normally UK will start creating BEF in about July 1936 with the outbreak of SCW.
###################
