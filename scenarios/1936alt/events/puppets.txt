##
#2400521 - IndiePrep. Has to fire before any automatic process takes over. Prerequisite for 2400522, 2400523, 2400524, 2400525.
#
#2400522 - Automatic Independent State init. Will fire if country revolted, released by player, OR by event. Will clear any puppet effects if they exist (2400535) and will also trigger forced peace (2400602) if legitIndie is not set.
#2400523 - Automatic Weak   Puppet init. Will fire if country is puppeted and puppetLevel1 flag is set, but not puppetLevel1made. Only triggers 2400536. Will ALSO fire if no puppetLevel flag is set at all, i.e. country made a puppet outside of events.
#2400524 - Automatic Medium Puppet init. Will fire if country is puppeted and puppetLevel2 flag is set, but not puppetLevel2made. Only triggers 2400537.
#2400525 - Automatic Strong Puppet init. Will fire if country is puppeted and puppetLevel3 flag is set, but not puppetLevel3made. Only triggers 2400538.
#
#2400530 - Setting the stayInWar flag to true. Country with the "stayInWar" flag will not quit the war if it ceases being a puppet.
#
#2400535 - Establishing Independent State. Can be triggered externally or by automatic 2400522. If country is already puppetLevel1, 2 or 3, will remove the effects and set flag to legitIndie.
#2400536 - Establishing Weak   Puppet. Can be triggered externally or by automatic 2400523. If country is already puppetLevel 2 or 3, will switch the effects and flags to puppetLevel1.
#2400537 - Establishing Medium Puppet. Can be triggered externally or by automatic 2400524. If country is already puppetLevel 1 or 3, will switch the effects and flags to puppetLevel2.
#2400538 - Establishing Strong Puppet. Can be triggered externally or by automatic 2400525. If country is already puppetLevel 1 or 2, will switch the effects and flags to puppetLevel3.
#
#2400540 - Weak   Puppet Dissent floor. Will keep dissent at the minimum of 35% (effective 40%).
#2400541 - Medium Puppet Dissent floor. Will keep dissent at the minimum of 15% (effective 20%).
#2400542 - Strong Puppet Dissent floor. Will keep dissent at the minimum of  1% (effective  5%).
#
#2400545 - Adds    the counter-AI-cheat penalties. Will force penalties if dissent gets too high (normally AI gets a pass).
#2400546 - Removes the counter-AI-cheat penalties. Will remove penalties if dissent is lowered, will ALSO fire if the former AI country becomes human controlled.
#
#2400600 - Resistance event chain starting point. Sets a timer to trigger 2400601 within a certain time period. 1 year for puppetLevel1, 2 years for puppetLevel2, 4 years for puppetLevel3
#2400601 - If still a puppet, one of 3 things will happen: 50% revolution fails (5% dissent gain); 25% revolution succeeds (will end puppet status and trigger 2400535 & 2400602 with possibility of war), 25% civil war (10% dissent and massive rebellions)
#2400602 - Break alliances, reset sliders, and set high neutrality. If still a puppet, will addcore on 1614 and trigger 2400603 and 2400604.
#2400603 - End puppet and removecore on 1614. Core is needed to help overlord identify the rebellious puppet.
#2400604 - The intervention - give the former overlord and option to declare war on the rebllious country identified by the 1614 core.
##
